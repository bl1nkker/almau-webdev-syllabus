Which class is right for you?

Attack Aircraft

The attack aircraft is the main class in the game and the main firepower of the squad. The attack aircraft occupies a leading position in the squad, it is entrusted with the task of destroying the enemy, so when playing for this class, you need to move and shoot a lot. Attack aircraft is a versatile class that will come in handy in any situation.

Medic

Medic is a classic utility class. He occupies a leading position in the squad. The Medic is a master of sliding and fighting in narrow corridors where it is almost impossible to dodge a shotgun blast, avoiding open spaces and large runs where he can become easy prey for a sniper or attack aircraft.

Engineer

The Engineer is a versatile class that would come in handy in any situation. It is especially effective in close range combat. The main weapons of this class are submachine guns and shortened machine guns. This is ideal for close to medium range combat.

Sniper

The sniper is the only class that can successfully hit enemies at long range, dealing significant damage to the enemy unit and suppressing the attack. Its task is the timely destruction of especially dangerous combat units of the enemy detachment - snipers and machine gunners, and in protracted battles - heavy infantrymen.
